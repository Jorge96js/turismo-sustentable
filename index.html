TURISMO SUSTENTABLE A WALK ON THE WILD SIDE

UNTAMED PATAGONIA

To walk on the wild side means to journey across the Lago Argentino, to discover the majesty alive within the Parque Nacional Los Glaciares. An invitation to let go and breathe purer air; to be one with nature and feel yourself a part of everything around you.

STAY

We invite you to come and discover a land of untouched nature, with treasures hidden in the trails of the glaciers as they carved and defined this land. Those who choose to stay and experience the enveloping night skies and expansive sunrises in this untamed land are those we call wild souls. Wild souls that come to stay.

PARQUE NACIONAL LOS GLACIARES

El escenario de esta aventura es un parque nacional que protege los glaciares del Campo de Hielo Patagónico Sur, un paisaje imponente en el que podés seguir las huellas reminiscentes de los pioneros que exploraron estas tierras australes.
